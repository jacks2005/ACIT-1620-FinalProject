The Fantastic Four: First Steps

Rating

Set against the vibrant backdrop of a 1960s-inspired, retro-futuristic world, Marvel Studios' "The Fantastic Four: First Steps" introduces Marvel's First Family--Reed Richards/Mister Fantastic (Pedro Pascal), Sue Storm/Invisible Woman (Vanessa Kirby), Johnny Storm/Human Torch (Joseph Quinn) and Ben Grimm/The Thing (Ebon Moss-Bachrach) as they face their most daunting challenge yet. Forced to balance their roles as heroes with the strength of their family bond, they must defend Earth from a ravenous space god called Galactus (Ralph Ineson) and his enigmatic Herald, Silver Surfer (Julia Garner). And if Galactus' plan to devour the entire planet and everyone on it weren't bad enough, it suddenly gets very personal.

Tron: Ares

Rating

Tron: Ares (2025) is a sci-fi action film set 15 years after Tron: Legacy. The story follows Ares, a highly advanced program (played by Jared Leto), who was created by the Dillinger System as a Master Control Program. He's tasked with obtaining the "permanence code" — a secret piece of software made by Kevin Flynn — but eventually questions his loyalties. Pulled into the real (human) world, Ares becomes fascinated by human emotions and existence, sparking a deeper journey into what it means to be alive.
